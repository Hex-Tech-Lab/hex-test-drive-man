## Download Challenges & Solutions

### Challenge 1: Egypt Website Authentication
**Issue**: The official Geely Egypt website (geelyautoegypt.com) uses session-based authentication for brochure downloads. Direct curl attempts returned 13-byte error responses.

**Solution**: Found alternative Egypt brochures hosted on geely.com.eg domain (older site) which allowed direct downloads without authentication.

**Result**: Successfully downloaded 2/7 Egypt-specific brochures (Coolray, Starray).

### Challenge 2: Missing Egypt Brochures
**Issue**: The following models had no publicly accessible Egypt brochures:
- Emgrand
- Okavango
- GX3 Pro
- Geometry C
- Cityray

**Solution**: Downloaded regional brochures from:
- **Philippines** (Emgrand, Okavango) - Similar market, right-hand drive
- **GCC Markets** (Bahrain, KSA, Qatar, Lebanon) - Same MENA region, similar specs
- All brochures are in English and contain full technical specifications

**Rationale**: Regional brochures are acceptable proxies because:
1. Geely maintains consistent specs across MENA/Asia markets
2. Egypt recently started local CKD production (January 2025) of Emgrand and Coolray
3. GCC/Philippines specs are nearly identical to Egypt models
